Something You Need To Know About Bangabazar

Kamalapur Railway station starting from 1 May 1968. Fulbaria was Dhaka's first railway station which is adjacent to Gulisthan.There a Market was developed near to fulbaria station in 1965, where hawkers and vendors bring headloads goods everyday and also somepeople sell snacks, drinks and some day-to-day usage goods. This is how Bangabazar actually created and now this is the largest wholesale and retail market for readymade garments. That's how bangazar established from hawkers market.
